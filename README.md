# Operational-Inteligent-Discovery-Project
Business analyst case study focused on operational intelligence and digital transformation. 

## Overview

This project documents an early-stage discovery and business analysis initiative for a fictional energy company (PT XYZ).

The objective was to understand operational challenges related to fragmented data sources and identify opportunities for improving decision-making through digital transformation and operational intelligence.

## Business Challenge

PT XYZ operated multiple systems containing operational and business information, including:

- Process monitoring systems
- Asset management systems
- Enterprise resource planning (ERP)
- Maintenance records
- Operational reports

Although large amounts of data were available, users often needed to access multiple systems to investigate incidents, understand asset performance, and support operational decisions.

## Key Findings

Several observations emerged during the discovery phase:

- Information was distributed across multiple platforms.
- Data existed, but actionable insights were difficult to obtain.
- Users spent significant time gathering information before making decisions.
- Faster analysis and operational visibility were identified as important business needs.
